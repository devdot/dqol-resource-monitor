## How can I migrate from YARM?

1. With YARM mod active, go to the YARM tab in the Resource Monitor menu.
2. Import sites from YARM (will import original amount, site name and age).
3. Disable YARM mod setting "Enable map markers" and wait for YARM map markers to disappear.
4. Disable YARM.

## How can I move a site after I moved the resources manually?

Since 1.6.0, this is possible through the area tool (open the site and click the area tool button).

## A mod changed a resource through a script but the site wasn't updated. What can I do?

Depending on what the mod changed, you may do either of the following things:

- Set the new resource area manually (open the site and click the area tool button).
- Create a new site with the new resource type (through the other tab) and then manually set the area (through the area tool in the new site's window).
- Reset and rescan the entire surface through the Surfaces tab (will reset all sites).

## Some modded resources are not being put into sites as I would expect. What can I do?

You may modify the settings that are used in site generation for each site in the Other tab of the menu. For example, if the sites of a resource are too fractured for your taste, make that resource loose and re-scan the surface.
